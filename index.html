Where can I vote early in Georgia's General Election?

  • Click on any polling place marker to view its title, address, and availability.
  • Select a county to view all early voting locations in that county.
  • Enter your location below and submit to view the closest 3 early voting locations near you.





For best results, we recommend using this site on a mobile device with a wide screen orientation.

All data for polling locations was fetched from Georgia's My Voter Page.
