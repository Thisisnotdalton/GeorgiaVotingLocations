Georgia Early Voting Locations

Data for polling places was fetched from the Georgia Secretary of State's Advance Voting Location Information tool.

This tool is not affiliated with any government agency or group. This page was last updated on October 13, 2024. Please direct all questions, suggestions, or corrections to the project's GitHub Issues page.

Looking for an early voting location?
  1. Provide your location or select a county to view early voting locations in that county.
  2. Select a time and day to filter early polling locations that are open.
  3. Click on a polling place (gray circles) to view its name, address, and hours.
  4. Click anywhere on the map to close this help message!
